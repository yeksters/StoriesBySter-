About the artist

The artist behind the stories

Y. Ster is an illustrator whose work lives at the intersection of myth, memory and the everyday. Rooted in Florence and shaped by a deep love of storytelling, each image carries its own inner world.

From ancient tragedy to the warmth of a Tuscan afternoon, the work spans worlds while remaining unmistakably human.
